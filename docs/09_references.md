## Clinical — Oncology and NSCLC

**[1]** Reck, M., Rodríguez-Abreu, D., Robinson, A.G., et al.
"Pembrolizumab versus Chemotherapy for PD-L1–Positive Non–Small-Cell
Lung Cancer." *New England Journal of Medicine*, 2016;375:1823–1833.
https://doi.org/10.1056/NEJMoa1606774
*(KEYNOTE-024 primary analysis — basis for pembrolizumab Category 1
recommendation for PD-L1 TPS >= 50% first-line NSCLC)*

**[2]** Reck, M., Rodríguez-Abreu, D., Robinson, A.G., et al.
"Five-Year Outcomes With Pembrolizumab Versus Chemotherapy for
Metastatic Non–Small-Cell Lung Cancer With PD-L1 Tumor Proportion
Score ≥ 50%." *Journal of Clinical Oncology*, 2021;39(21):2339–2349.
https://doi.org/10.1200/JCO.21.00174
*(KEYNOTE-024 5-year follow-up — 31.9% vs 16.3% OS, HR 0.63)*

**[3]** Mok, T.S.K., Wu, Y.-L., Kudaba, I., et al.
"Pembrolizumab versus chemotherapy for previously untreated,
PD-L1-expressing, locally advanced or metastatic non-small-cell
lung cancer (KEYNOTE-042)."
*The Lancet*, 2019;393(10183):1819–1830.
https://doi.org/10.1016/S0140-6736(18)32409-7
*(KEYNOTE-042 — extends indication to PD-L1 TPS >= 1%,
Category 2A for 1-49%, Category 1 for >= 50%)*

**[4]** Gandhi, L., Rodríguez-Abreu, D., Gadgeel, S., et al.
"Pembrolizumab plus Chemotherapy in Metastatic Non–Small-Cell Lung
Cancer." *New England Journal of Medicine*, 2018;378:2078–2092.
https://doi.org/10.1056/NEJMoa1801005
*(KEYNOTE-189 — pembrolizumab + pemetrexed + platinum
for non-squamous NSCLC, any PD-L1)*

**[5]** Paz-Ares, L., Luft, A., Vicente, D., et al.
"Pembrolizumab plus Chemotherapy for Squamous Non–Small-Cell Lung
Cancer." *New England Journal of Medicine*, 2018;379:2040–2051.
https://doi.org/10.1056/NEJMoa1810865
*(KEYNOTE-407 — pembrolizumab + carboplatin + paclitaxel/nab-paclitaxel
for squamous NSCLC, any PD-L1)*

**[6]** Tsao, M.S., Kerr, K.M., Kockx, M., et al.
"PD-L1 Immunohistochemistry Comparability Study in Real-Life Clinical
Samples: Results of Blueprint Phase 2 Project."
*Journal of Thoracic Oncology*, 2018;13(9):1302–1311.
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jtho.2018.05.013
*(Blueprint Phase 2 — 22C3 vs SP142 vs SP263 vs 28-8 assay comparison;
why assay type matters for PA)*

**[7]** Ettinger, D.S., Wood, D.E., Aisner, D.L., et al.
"Non–Small Cell Lung Cancer, Version 5.2026."
*NCCN Clinical Practice Guidelines in Oncology.*
National Comprehensive Cancer Network, March 13, 2026.
https://www.nccn.org/guidelines/guidelines-detail?category=1&id=1450
*(Primary clinical authority — Category 1 pembrolizumab recommendation,
biomarker testing requirements, staging criteria)*

**[8]** Oken, M.M., Creech, R.H., Tormey, D.C., et al.
"Toxicity and response criteria of the Eastern Cooperative Oncology
Group." *American Journal of Clinical Oncology*, 1982;5(6):649–655.
*(Original ECOG PS scale publication — basis for ECOG 0-1
eligibility requirement in KEYNOTE-024)*

**[9]** Sosa, E., Wei, L., Gong, Y., et al.
"Impact of Treatment Delays on Survival in Stage III Non-Small Cell
Lung Cancer Treated with Definitive Chemoradiation."
*Journal of Thoracic Oncology*, 2019;14(2):268–275.
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jtho.2018.11.028
*(14-day delay and mortality risk — clinical urgency behind PA problem)*
